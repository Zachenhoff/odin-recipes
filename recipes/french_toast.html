Fluffy French Toast

Description

This French Toast recipe will make the most tasty fluffy French Toast you will ever have! It's secret ingredient is all-purpose flour, which binds the liquids together, creating a more traditional 'batter,' helping prevent sogginess. All-purpose flour makes sure the French toast is crispy on the outside, but fluffy and soft on the inside

Home

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices bread

Steps

  1. Start by gathering all the materials
  2. In a large mixing bowl, add the flour first. Gradually stir in the milk, then mix in the eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until everything is well blended.
  3. Lightly oil a griddle/frying pan and put it over medium heat. While it warms up, dip the bread slices into the milk mixture until they're soaked through.
  4. Place the slices on the hot pan, cooking them in batches, until each side turns golden brown.
  5. Serve while hot with extra toppings, and enjoy!
