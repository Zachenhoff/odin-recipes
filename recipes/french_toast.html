Fluffy French Toast

Description

This French Toast recipe will make the most tasty fluffy French Toast you will ever have! It's secret ingredient is all-purpose flour, which binds the liquids together, creating a more traditional 'batter,' helping prevent sogginess. All-purpose flour makes sure the French toast is crispy on the outside, but fluffy and soft on the inside

Home

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices bread
