Brownies

Description

This recipe makes tasty, rich, and fudgy brownies. These brownies are moist and also super quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup self-rising flour
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven 175 degrees Celsius (and 350 degrees F for the American viewers). Grease, then flour an 9x9 or 8x8-inch baking pan.
  2. Mix sugar and butter in a medium bowl. Add eggs and mix well. Combine flour, cocoa, and salt and stir into sugar mixture.
  3. Mix in vanilla, then stir in the walnuts (optional). Spread nice and evenly into the pan.
  4. Bake mixture in the preheated oven until edges are firm (25 to 30 minutes). Cool before cutting into squares and serving!
